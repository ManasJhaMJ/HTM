GUIDE TO PLAY DRUM

Use your computer keyboard or click the drum kit to play the virtual drums. You can use the following shortcuts:

  • Crash cymbal : y
  • Ride cymbal : u
  • Hi-hat (closed) : r
  • Hi-hat (open) : e
  • Hi-hat (foot) : c
  • High tom : g
  • Low tom : h
  • Floor tom : j
  • Snare Drum : s
  • Snare drum (cross stick) : d
